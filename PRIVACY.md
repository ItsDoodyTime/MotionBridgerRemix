## Privacy & Usage Notice

This tool processes webcam input locally on your own computer in order to perform real-time motion tracking.

- All camera data is processed entirely on-device
- No video, images, facial data, or biometric information are recorded or stored
- No data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared over the internet
- No analytics, telemetry, logging, or tracking of any kind is performed

This software does not collect, store, or have access to any personal data.
All processing happens locally using open-source libraries.

Any data generated by this tool remains entirely under your control.
