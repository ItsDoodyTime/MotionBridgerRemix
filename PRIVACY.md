## Privacy & Usage Notice

This tool processes webcam input locally on your computer to perform real-time motion tracking.<br>
- All camera data is processed **entirely in memory and on-device**.
- No video, images, facial data, or biometric information are recorded or stored.
- No data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared over the internet.
- The application may generate local error and diagnostic logs to help with debugging and stability.
- These logs **do not contain** camera images, facial data, personal/device information, or biometric data.

All processing happens locally using open-source libraries.<br>
Any data generated by this tool remains **entirely under your control**.
